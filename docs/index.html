🌲 Pacific Yew Habitat Map

35 study areas across BC's Coastal Western Hemlock (CWH) zone.
MLP model · 10 m GEE satellite embeddings · 2024 imagery.

About

Predicted probability of Pacific yew (Taxus brevifolia) habitat using a neural network trained on iNaturalist observations and Google Earth Engine satellite embeddings. Each tile covers ~10×10 km.

Use the observation panel (top right) to report yew sightings and improve the model.
